Our Story

Made with Faith,
Crafted with Purpose

Truth and Tallow was born from a family's desire to live closer to the way God designed us — nourished by the earth, free from synthetic chemicals, and rooted in truth.

We discovered the power of grass-fed tallow — one of nature's most nutrient-dense skincare ingredients — and knew we had to share it. Every product we craft is made in small batches with organic, traceable ingredients that are safe enough to eat.

Our motto says it all: "If you shouldn't eat it, you shouldn't wear it." We believe your skin deserves the same standard of purity as the food on your table.

🌿 Pure Essential Oils
